Drum kit pads
Touch performance
A compact performance kit that uses the same synthetic Pocket Chordsmith drum bus. Kick, snare, hat and open hat can write into the current rhythm grid while playback is running. Clap maps to a snare accent. Toms, crash and ride are live-only colour.
Beat sequencer
Resolution: Full
Advanced rhythm tools
Beat cells cycle Off -> Hit -> Accent. Full/Half/Quarter/Eighth/Sixteenth are straight grid resolutions. Triplets are now marked as tuplets: place two adjacent hits, then drag right-to-left across them to mark a small 3. The two cells then play three evenly spaced hits in the time of two cells. Triplets cannot overlap; consecutive triplets use pairs like 1-2, 3-4, 5-6.
Guitar rhythm
Cells cycle Rest, strum, palm mute, accent, hold and scratch. Guitar follows each bar's chord as root, fifth and octave power chords.
Melody sequencer
Pitch Ready
Drag to play the selected melody track. X controls pitch. In Sustain mode, Y controls sustain or frequency. In Pulse mode, Y can control pulse rate in time with the music.
Tap empty cell to place current note. Tap filled cell to cycle upward. Drag right from a note to hold it across steps. Long press to clear. Extra melody tracks are available in Advanced mode.
Parts
Build up to eight sections, set each section length, then arrange them into a song sequence.
Song sequence
Tap a slot to cycle A-H. Long press clears it. Use + Slot to extend the song. Export can render the full song sequence.

FX
Live playback FX shape the chord and melody bus only. FX Mix now controls the overall wet level, so changes should be much easier to hear on phone speakers. Exports stay dry for now.
Advanced playback
Humanise adds tiny timing and level variation. Pump ducks chords around kick hits for a sidechain feel.
Lofi / Chill
Texture is procedural and subtle. Turn it off for clean exports or performance checks.
Interface
Manual bass uses tap to cycle notes, drag right to hold or glide, then long press to accent. Long press an accented note again to clear it.
