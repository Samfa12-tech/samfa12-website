Pocket Audio tools

Pocket Audio is a family of browser-based music tools for sketching songs, exporting ideas, live remixing, and bringing adaptive music into games.

The public source home connects Pocket Chordsmith, Pocket DJ, Pocket DAW experiments, the MIT-licensed Godot addon, and shared game-audio tooling.
